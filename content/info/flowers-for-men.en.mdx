## What makes a bouquet feel right for men?

There is no single rule, but these choices usually land well:

- **Stronger colours** — yellow, orange, deep red, white, and green feel fresh rather than delicate
- **Sculptural blooms** — orchids and structured stems look modern and intentional on a desk or shelf
- **Cleaner shapes** — structured bouquets or single-flower styles read as modern, not fussy
- **Occasion-first messaging** — celebrate an achievement, wish someone well, or say thank you instead of leaning on romance alone
- **Understated size** — a well-made medium bouquet often feels more natural than an oversized romantic display
