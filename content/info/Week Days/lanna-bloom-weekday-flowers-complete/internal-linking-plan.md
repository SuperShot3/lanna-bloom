# Internal Linking Plan

## Pillar page

The pillar page links to every weekday article using descriptive anchors.

## Every weekday article

Add links to:

1. Pillar page: `Thai weekday flower colors`
2. Previous weekday article
3. Next weekday article
4. Relevant color/product collection
5. Delivery information
6. Custom-order/contact page

## Navigation cycle

Sunday → Monday → Tuesday → Wednesday → Thursday → Friday → Saturday → Sunday

## Monday rule

Do not create a duplicate route. Patch the existing Monday article and preserve its current slug.
