## How online checkout works

When you **buy flowers online** at Lanna Bloom:

1. **Browse the catalog** and open the bouquet you want.
2. **Choose size** and add the bouquet to your cart.
3. **Enter delivery details** — recipient name, address, date, and any notes for the driver.
4. **Review your order** and continue to secure checkout.
5. **Pay online by card** through Stripe checkout.
6. **Our local team prepares and delivers** your bouquet within our Chiang Mai delivery area.

Major credit and debit cards are welcome at checkout. That is especially helpful if you are outside Thailand or simply want to finish payment immediately after choosing a bouquet.

For delivery zones, fees, and timing, see our [delivery policy](/en/info/delivery-policy). For faster timing questions, see [same-day flower delivery in Chiang Mai](/en/info/same-day-flower-delivery-chiang-mai).

## Choosing the right bouquet online

Not sure what to pick? Start with the occasion, then narrow by colour and style.

- **Romantic moments:** red or pink roses — browse [rose bouquets in Chiang Mai](/en/collections/roses-chiang-mai)
- **Birthdays and cheerful gifts:** sunflowers or bright mixed bouquets
- **Elegant surprises:** soft pink, white, or cream-toned arrangements
- **Bigger statements:** larger rose counts such as our [51 roses guide](/en/info/51-roses-chiang-mai)

If two bouquets look similar online, choose the one that best matches the recipient's taste rather than the biggest arrangement on the page.
