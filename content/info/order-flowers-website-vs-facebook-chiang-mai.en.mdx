## Facebook is useful for discovery

- look at recent posts and photos
- get a first impression of a brand
- follow updates and seasonal bouquets

## But websites are better for structured ordering

- browse bouquets in one place
- compare styles and prices side by side
- review delivery and order details clearly
- follow a structured checkout — without back-and-forth
