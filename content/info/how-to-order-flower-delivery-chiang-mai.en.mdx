**Standard bouquets:** You do **not** pay in chat for catalog items. On our [website](/en/catalog) you add to cart, enter delivery details, then **Pay now** and finish on **Stripe Checkout**. Successful payment **confirms your paid order**—checkout gives us address, phone, and delivery notes so we can fulfill without a follow-up chat. **If you enter an email at checkout**, our system sends you an **order confirmation email** with your order ID, delivery recap, line items, total, and a link to **view full order details** online (same path as the page you’re sent back to after Stripe). You are **not** expected to message us afterwards; [LINE / WhatsApp](/en/contact) is **optional** if you want to add a note or ask something. We may **contact you** (call or chat) only if we cannot find the address or need to clarify delivery—so a correct phone number helps. **Please add a Google Maps link** in the optional field at checkout when you can; it makes a real difference for the driver. Stripe may show **cards**, **PromptPay** (when enabled), wallets, etc.—**never cash** online. For **custom work**, you may [start in chat](/en/contact) first.

## Overview

1. Choose a bouquet on the [website](/en/catalog)
2. Add to cart and complete checkout — **email** (recommended, for confirmation mail), **address**, date/time, driver notes, and **strongly recommended:** a **Google Maps link** (share URL from the Maps app)
3. Click **Pay now** → **Stripe Checkout** — pay with the methods Stripe shows
4. After payment, Stripe sends you back to our site and your order is confirmed; **if you gave an email at checkout**, we also **email you** the confirmation (order number, summary, and link to your order — you don’t need to copy anything from the screen unless you want to)
5. **Messaging us is optional** — only if you want to add something. We may contact **you** if we need help finding the place
6. Delivery during working hours 08:00–20:00

## Step-by-step

### 1) Order and pay on the website

Browse [bouquets](/en/catalog), add what you need to the cart, then complete checkout with:

- Recipient name (and phone if available)
- **Your email (recommended)** — we send the **order confirmation** here after payment (order ID, delivery summary, items, total, link to your order page)
- Delivery address (home / condo / hotel / hospital)
- **Google Maps link (optional but strongly recommended)** — in checkout, paste a share link so the pin matches the drop-off; Open Google Maps → drop a pin / choose the place → Share → copy link → paste into the field
- Delivery date and preferred time window
- Note for the driver (building, floor, room, landmarks, parking)
- Message card text (optional)

When the form is complete, click **Pay now**. You’ll be redirected to **Stripe Checkout** and pick the option you want from the methods Stripe offers on that page. After payment succeeds, Stripe returns you to our **order confirmation** page with your reference and status. **If you entered an email**, you’ll also get our **confirmation email** with the same essentials and a **view order** link—check inbox (and spam) for **“Order confirmation … — Lanna Bloom”**. If you skip email, rely on the page we show after return and your phone for any follow-up from us.

### 2) Do you have to message us after paying?

**No.** Checkout + Stripe payment is enough. We use what you entered (and your phone) to run the order. You **do not** need to open LINE or WhatsApp just to “confirm” a standard web order.

**When we might contact you:** If the address is unclear, the pin is wrong, or the driver cannot reach the building, we may **call or message you** using the number you provided—please answer if you can.

**If you want to reach us anyway** (extra note, timing question, special request): [LINE](/en/contact) or [WhatsApp](/en/contact) — include your **order number or link** so we can find you quickly.

**Best thing for delivery:** Put **driver notes** and a **Google Maps link** in checkout up front—that usually prevents issues before we need to call.

## Same-day delivery and the 18:00 cutoff

We deliver during working hours 08:00–20:00. Same-day delivery is possible when ordered early (subject to bouquet availability and delivery capacity).

Orders placed after 18:00 may be scheduled for the next day. Sometimes same-day is still possible, but it depends on stock and the delivery schedule.

If it's urgent, complete checkout as early as you can; add a **Google Maps link** and clear driver notes so we can go straight to the pin.

## Payment

How you pay depends on whether you use **Stripe Checkout** on the website or arrange payment **in chat**.

| | **Stripe Checkout** (click **Pay now** on the cart) | **LINE / WhatsApp** (when we coordinate with you) |
|--|------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| **What it is** | Stripe’s hosted payment page — same flow for standard bouquet orders | We send **PromptPay QR** or **Thai bank transfer** details in chat after we agree on the order |
| **Cash** | **No** — Stripe does not offer cash for online checkout | **No** — we give you PromptPay or bank transfer details instead |
| **Cards (Visa, Mastercard, etc.)** | **Yes**, when shown on the Stripe page | — |
| **Thai PromptPay** | **Yes**, when **PromptPay** appears on the Stripe page (Stripe supports it for Thailand in THB when it is enabled for our account) | **Yes**, when we send you our QR / instructions in chat |
| **Apple Pay / Google Pay** | **Sometimes**, if Stripe shows them for your browser or device | — |
| **Manual Thai bank transfer** | Only if Stripe lists a supported bank/transfer method on the page | **Yes**, when we send account details in chat |

**Notes**

- **Stripe** chooses which methods appear based on your payment in **THB**, your situation, and which methods are enabled for our business. You may see **cards only**, or **cards plus PromptPay and wallets** — pick what Stripe shows on that page.
- **Cash** is not an option on Stripe or our online checkout.

**Custom or special cases**

- For [custom orders](/en/custom-order) or if checkout is difficult, [message us](/en/contact) and we can agree payment (for example PromptPay or bank transfer in chat).

## FAQ

### 1) I paid on the website — what should I do next?

**Nothing required.** After Stripe confirms payment, your order is in the system. **Check your email** for our confirmation (if you provided one at checkout), or open the **order page** from the link in that email or from the page you landed on after Stripe. Message us **only if** you want to change something or ask a question—include your order number or link. We may contact **you** if we need help with the address.

### 2) Can I edit delivery details after ordering?

Often yes, if we haven't started preparation or dispatched the driver. Message us with your order number or link as soon as possible.

### 3) Can you deliver to hotels, condos, or hospitals?

Yes. Please include room or ward details if available, plus any access rules (lobby, reception, gate code). A **Google Maps link** to the correct entrance helps a lot. Hospitals may have delivery restrictions—put details in checkout and we’ll contact you if something can’t be done.

### 4) Can I choose an exact delivery time?

You can choose a preferred time window. Exact timing depends on traffic and delivery routing, but we follow your window as closely as possible.

### 5) Can I add a message card?

Yes. Add it at checkout or send the text in chat. Keep it clear and short for the card.

### 6) What if the driver has trouble finding the address?

This is why **Google Maps link + written address + driver notes** in checkout matter—we try to fix it before dispatch. If something is still unclear, we **contact you** on the phone number you gave. You can also message us if you want to add a landmark after ordering.

### 7) What proof of payment do you need?

For payments completed **through Stripe Checkout**, your order page and **confirmation email** (when you gave an email) reflect payment after Stripe confirms. You usually **do not** need to send a bank slip. **PromptPay** or card payments made **on Stripe’s page** are confirmed by Stripe automatically. If we asked you to pay **only in chat** (manual transfer), send the slip or screenshot there. If something looks wrong, message us with your order number or link.

### 8) Changes, cancellations, refunds

If something changes, message us as soon as you can with your order number or link. Policy details are here: [Refund / Replacement / Cancellation Policy](/en/refund-replacement).
