Ordering with Lanna Bloom starts on our website. After **Pay now**, you complete payment on **Stripe Checkout** (Stripe’s hosted page). Stripe can offer **more than cards** — for example major **credit and debit cards** (Visa, Mastercard, etc.), **PromptPay** in Thailand, and **Apple Pay** or **Google Pay** where Stripe supports them — depending on what we have **enabled with Stripe**, your currency (**THB**), and your device. **Cash is not accepted** through Stripe or the website. When paying by card, card details are entered only on Stripe’s page; we do not collect or store your full card number on our site.

After you complete payment, please [contact us on LINE or WhatsApp](/en/contact) and send your order number or order link. We'll confirm availability and delivery details there, and keep everything in one place.

Sending your order number or link helps us find your order instantly and coordinate smoothly — especially if we need a quick confirmation from you or the driver needs help with delivery details.

For **custom requests** and special occasions, you can start in chat. For standard bouquets, we recommend starting with the website checkout.

## Overview

1. Choose a bouquet on the [website](/en/catalog)
2. Add to cart and complete checkout details (recipient, address, date/time, note, message card)
3. Click **Pay now** → **Stripe Checkout** opens — choose from the payment methods Stripe shows (cards, PromptPay, wallets, etc., depending on availability)
4. After successful payment, keep your order number + order link from the order confirmation page
5. [Contact us in chat](/en/contact) to confirm availability and final delivery details
6. Delivery during working hours 08:00–20:00

## Step-by-step

### 1) Order and pay on the website

Browse [bouquets](/en/catalog), add what you need to the cart, then complete checkout with:

- Recipient name (and phone if available)
- Delivery address (home / condo / hotel / hospital)
- Delivery date and preferred time window
- Note for the driver (building, floor, room, landmarks, parking)
- Message card text (optional)

When the form is complete, click **Pay now**. You’ll be redirected to **Stripe Checkout** and pick the option you want from the methods Stripe offers on that page. After payment succeeds, you’ll have an order number and an order link on your order page.

### 2) Contact us to confirm

Next, message us here:

- [Contact on LINE](/en/contact)
- [Contact on WhatsApp](/en/contact)

Please send your order link or order number first.

This is how we find your order instantly and confirm everything without confusion.

## Same-day delivery and the 18:00 cutoff

We deliver during working hours 08:00–20:00. Same-day delivery is possible when ordered early (subject to bouquet availability and delivery capacity).

Orders placed after 18:00 may be scheduled for the next day. Sometimes same-day is still possible, but it depends on stock and the delivery schedule.

If it's urgent, complete checkout and [message us](/en/contact) right away with your order number or link.

## Payment

How you pay depends on whether you use **Stripe Checkout** on the website or arrange payment **in chat**.

| | **Stripe Checkout** (click **Pay now** on the cart) | **LINE / WhatsApp** (when we coordinate with you) |
|--|------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| **What it is** | Stripe’s hosted payment page — same flow for standard bouquet orders | We send **PromptPay QR** or **Thai bank transfer** details in chat after we agree on the order |
| **Cash** | **No** — Stripe does not offer cash for online checkout | **No** — we give you PromptPay or bank transfer details instead |
| **Cards (Visa, Mastercard, etc.)** | **Yes**, when shown on the Stripe page | — |
| **Thai PromptPay** | **Yes**, when **PromptPay** appears on the Stripe page (Stripe supports it for Thailand in THB when it is enabled for our account) | **Yes**, when we send you our QR / instructions in chat |
| **Apple Pay / Google Pay** | **Sometimes**, if Stripe shows them for your browser or device | — |
| **Manual Thai bank transfer** | Only if Stripe lists a supported bank/transfer method on the page | **Yes**, when we send account details in chat |

**Notes**

- **Stripe** chooses which methods appear based on your payment in **THB**, your situation, and which methods are enabled for our business. You may see **cards only**, or **cards plus PromptPay and wallets** — pick what Stripe shows on that page.
- **Cash** is not an option on Stripe or our online checkout.

**Custom or special cases**

- For [custom orders](/en/custom-order) or if checkout is difficult, [message us](/en/contact) and we can agree payment (for example PromptPay or bank transfer in chat).

## FAQ

### 1) I placed an order — what should I do next?

[Message us on LINE or WhatsApp](/en/contact) and send your order link or order number. We'll confirm availability and final delivery details. If you just finished Stripe checkout, your payment is already processing or completed; you’ll see status on your order page.

### 2) Can I edit delivery details after ordering?

Often yes, if we haven't started preparation or dispatched the driver. Message us with your order number or link as soon as possible.

### 3) Can you deliver to hotels, condos, or hospitals?

Yes. Please include room or ward details if available, plus any access rules (lobby, reception, gate code). Hospitals may have delivery restrictions, so confirmation in chat is important.

### 4) Can I choose an exact delivery time?

You can choose a preferred time window. Exact timing depends on traffic and delivery routing, but we follow your window as closely as possible.

### 5) Can I add a message card?

Yes. Add it at checkout or send the text in chat. Keep it clear and short for the card.

### 6) What if the driver has trouble finding the address?

This is why we ask for driver notes and a phone number when possible. We'll coordinate in chat and help the driver reach the correct location.

### 7) What proof of payment do you need?

For payments completed **through Stripe Checkout**, your order page updates after Stripe confirms the payment. You usually **do not** need to send a bank slip. **PromptPay** or card payments made **on Stripe’s page** are confirmed by Stripe automatically. If we asked you to pay **only in chat** (manual transfer), send the slip or screenshot there. If something looks wrong, message us with your order number or link.

### 8) Changes, cancellations, refunds

If something changes, message us as soon as you can with your order number or link. Policy details are here: [Refund / Replacement / Cancellation Policy](/en/refund-replacement).
