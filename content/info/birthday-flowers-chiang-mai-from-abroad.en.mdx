**Same-day delivery** may be possible in some cases, but it should not be assumed in every situation. Delivery depends on **timing**, **area**, and **availability**. **Clear timing communication** is better than unrealistic promises.

Some flowers are **seasonal** or may not always be available exactly as shown. A trustworthy service should communicate this **clearly** instead of promising an exact visual match in every case.
