## Why Tuesday is pink in Thai culture

Thai weekday traditions associate each day with a color and a planetary or divine figure. Tuesday is commonly represented by pink.

For international readers, it is useful to view this as a cultural color association rather than a strict rule. Pink may be used strongly in clothing or decorations, or simply included as a small symbolic accent.

A flower gift makes the idea easy to personalize. Soft blush flowers can feel gentle and romantic, while vivid pink blooms create a cheerful birthday surprise.
